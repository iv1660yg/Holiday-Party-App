Minute to Win It Team-Building Games

Overview

Engage in exciting "Minute to Win It" games designed to promote teamwork and collaboration! These fast-paced activities require you to partner with coworkers you don’t often interact with. Challenge yourself and have fun while building stronger connections.

Games

Pretty Windy 2v2

Objective: Each player knocks over five plastic cups using only the air from an inflated balloon.

Setup: Line up 10 plastic cups on each side of a table (5 per player). Each player gets one balloon.

Gameplay:

  • Each team has one minute to blow up their balloons and use the air to knock down the cups.
  • Each player must knock over five cups.
  • Players can blow up the balloon as many times as needed.

Winning: The team that knocks down all their cups first wins!

Cup Stack

Objective: Build a tower of 36 plastic cups within one minute.

Setup: Provide each team with 36 sturdy plastic cups.

Gameplay:

  • Each team has one minute to stack their cups into a stable tower.
  • Team members can collaborate to build the tower.
  • The tower must stand on its own without falling.

Winning: The team that completes their tower in the least time wins!

Moving On Up

Objective: Each team must move a designated cup from the bottom of the stack to the top within one minute.

Setup:Provide each team with 20 cups of the same color and one cup of a different color. The different-colored cup starts at the bottom of the stack.

Gameplay:

  • Each team has one minute to move the different-colored cup from the bottom to the top of the stack.
  • Team members must alternate turns, using one hand at a time to move the cups.
  • The cups must be moved one at a time.

Winning: The team that successfully moves the different-colored cup to the top of the stack first wins.

Penny Stack

Objective: Stack as many pennies as possible using only one hand.

Setup: Provide each team with a bowl of pennies and an open desk.

Gameplay:

  • Each team has one minute to stack as many pennies as possible.
  • Players can only use one hand to stack the pennies.

Winning: The team with the most pennies stacked within the time limit wins!

Fork It Over

Objective: Trap as many quarters as possible in the tines of a fork within one minute.

Setup: Provide each team with a fork and a stack of quarters.

Gameplay:

  • Each team has one minute to roll quarters and trap them in the fork tines.
  • Players must use their non-dominant hand to roll the quarters.

Winning: The team that traps the most quarters in the fork wins!

Rules

  • Each game lasts one minute.
  • Teams must consist of at least 2 players, and members should come from different departments or roles.
  • Points are awarded for task completion and creativity.
  • The team with the most points across all games wins!
