Holiday Bingo - How to Play

Welcome to the fun and festive Holiday Bingo game!

Overview

Welcome to Holiday Bingo! This interactive game brings the fun of traditional Bingo with a holiday twist. Play with friends, family, or colleagues and see who can complete their Bingo card first! Each card is filled with unique holiday-themed prompts that encourage interaction with others.

It’s a great way to mingle, share holiday traditions, and have fun during the holiday season.

How to Play

  1. Get Your Bingo Card: Upon starting the game, you’ll receive a randomly generated 5x5 Bingo card filled with holiday-themed prompts (e.g., "Find someone who has baked holiday cookies").
  2. Interact with Others: To fill out the squares, find someone who matches the prompt and have them enter their name in the corresponding square. Each person can only be used once per card.
  3. Check for Bingo: The goal is to complete a full row, column, or diagonal. Each Bingo line (row, column, or diagonal) is worth 1 point. There are a total of 12 possible Bingos on a 5x5 card.
  4. Submit Your Card: Once your card is complete, submit it for scoring. You can only submit 2 Bingo cards per player.
  5. Leaderboard: After submitting, your score will be added to the Holiday Bingo Leaderboard!

Game Rules

  • Name Uniqueness: Each name on your Bingo card must be unique. The same person cannot be listed more than once on your card. If you reuse a name, you will receive an error message and will need to update your card.
  • Case-Insensitive: Names are case-insensitive. For example, "Sam" and "sam" are treated as the same name.
  • Empty Squares: You can leave squares empty if you're unable to find someone who matches the prompt. However, rows, columns, or diagonals with empty squares won't count for Bingo.
  • Submission Limit: Each player is allowed to submit a maximum of 2 Bingo cards. If you try to submit more, you will be notified that you have reached your limit.
  • Scoring: A completed row, column, or diagonal is worth 1 point. There are 12 possible Bingos on the card. Your final score will be based on the number of completed lines (rows, columns, and diagonals).
  • Winning: The player with the most Bingos at the end of the event wins the game!
  • Fair Play: Be respectful and play fair. Make sure not to reuse names or try to cheat the system. Let's keep it fun for everyone!
