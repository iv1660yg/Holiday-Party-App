How Scoring Works

Your rank is based on a composite score that considers how close your predicted score was to your actual score (Accuracy Score) and how many questions you answered correctly (Performance Score).

Formula: Composite Score = (Accuracy Score × 0.4) + (Performance Score × 0.6)
