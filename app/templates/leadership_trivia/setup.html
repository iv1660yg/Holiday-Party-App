How Well Do You Know Your Leadership Team?

Objective

Test your knowledge of the leadership team! You'll answer a set of multiple-choice questions about the leaders, and your score will be determined by how many correct answers you give.

To get started

  • Enter your name to start.
  • Select the number of questions you want to attempt (10, 15, 20, 25, or 30).
  • Predict how many questions you think you'll answer correctly.
  • Answer each question by selecting the correct leader from a list of options.
  • After each question, you'll see whether your answer was correct or incorrect, along with the correct answer.
  • Complete all the questions to see your final score and your rank on the leaderboard.
