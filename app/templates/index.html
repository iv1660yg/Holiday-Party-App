Welcome to the Holiday Fun

Get Ready for a Fun-Filled Holiday Celebration!

The holidays are here, and it's time to have some fun and get to know your coworkers better! We’ve got exciting games and activities to bring everyone together and spread some festive cheer.

Here's What You Can Look Forward To:

Bingo Game

Ready for a fun challenge? The Bingo Game will have you talking to your peers to find out who’s got the best holiday memories, baked cookies, or holiday sweaters. It’s all about getting to know your coworkers and filling out your bingo card with their names to win!

Trivia Games

Test your knowledge with our Trivia Games! We have themed quizzes ranging from general holiday knowledge to a special trivia game where you’ll get to know the leadership team better. Think you know them all? It’s time to find out!

Voting

Let’s have some friendly competition with our Voting options! Vote for your favorites in categories like:

  • Best Potluck Food
  • Best Holiday Treat
  • Best Side Dish
  • Best Board Game
  • Best Holiday Attire

Who will be crowned the winner in each category? The results are in your hands!

Join the fun, make memories with your team, and be a part of the holiday celebration!
